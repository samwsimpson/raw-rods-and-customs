‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why not just include paint in the price?

A: Paint adds $20K-$30K to the cost and is highly personal. By delivering primer-ready, customers choose their own paint shop, color, and finish level‚Äîsaving money and getting exactly what they want. Many customers already have relationships with local body shops.

Q: How long is the build process?

A: 4-6 months from deposit to delivery. Timeline depends on kit availability from Factory Five and any custom requests. We provide weekly photo updates throughout the build.

Q: Can I register this vehicle in Texas?

A: Yes! Texas has favorable kit car laws. After delivery, you'll complete a safety inspection and submit Form VTR-68-A (Assembled Vehicle Statement) to the DMV. We provide all documentation needed. No emissions test required in most counties.

Q: What if something breaks after I take delivery?

A: Our 30-day shake-down warranty covers any assembly-related issues. The Cleveland PAP powertrain includes their 12-month/12,000-mile warranty that transfers to you. We also offer ongoing service and support.

Q: Can I choose a different engine?

A: Yes! We primarily use Cleveland PAP's Coyote 5.0 turnkey pallet, but we can also install LS3, LT1, or other turnkey systems. Different engines may affect pricing and timeline. Contact us to discuss options.

Q: Do you offer financing?

A: We require a $5,000 deposit to start, with the balance due at delivery. We can recommend specialty lenders who finance kit cars and custom builds. Many customers use home equity lines or personal loans.

Q: Can I visit during the build?

A: Absolutely! We encourage customers to visit the shop and see their build in progress. Schedule visits in advance so we can walk you through the process and answer questions.

Q: What makes turnkey pallets better than junkyard engines?

A: Turnkey pallets arrive pre-wired with ECU, harness, and all accessories tested and ready to install. This eliminates 40+ hours of troubleshooting, prevents compatibility issues, and includes a factory warranty. Junkyard engines are a gamble‚Äîwe eliminate that risk.
